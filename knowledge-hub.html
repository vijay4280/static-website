    Expert Answers to Your Irrigation Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why is my dripper not giving proper water?

    Reasons may be:

    • Clogging due to dirt
    • Low or high pressure
    • Pipe bending
    • Air-lock in the system

    Solution: Clean the filter regularly and flush the laterals.

    What is a venturi injector? Why is it used?

    A venturi injector is a device used to apply fertilizers through the irrigation system. It works on pressure differences and saves time & labor.

    What is the difference between sprinkler and rain gun?

    Sprinkler: Used for small to medium fields; uniform water distribution.

    Rain Gun: Covers large areas (up to 60–80 meters radius) and is useful for crops like sugarcane, fodder, groundnut, potato.

    How to prevent clogging in drip systems?
    • Clean sand & screen filters weekly
    • Flush lateral pipes
    • Use clean water
    • Use fertigation only with proper filtration
    • Install air release valves
    Why are filters important in irrigation systems?

    Filters remove sand, mud, and other particles. Without filters, drippers and nozzles get blocked easily.

    What are micro sprinklers?

    Micro sprinklers are small sprinklers used for nurseries, orchards, and vegetables. They save water and give gentle irrigation.

    Do drip systems increase crop yield?

    Yes! Drip irrigation provides:

    • Uniform water
    • No water stress
    • Efficient fertigation

    Overall yield can increase by 20–60%.

    Why is my pipe bursting or leaking?

    Possible reasons:

    • High pressure
    • Poor quality pipes
    • Wrong installation
    • Not using clamps or grommets properly

    Solution: Use ISI-quality products and proper fittings.

    What is an air release valve? Why do we need it?

    It releases trapped air from pipes during irrigation.

    • Prevents bursting
    • Improves water flow
    • Protects pump and pipelines
    Does drip irrigation reduce electricity cost?

    Yes! It requires less water and shorter pump running time, saving 30–50% electricity.

    Can I use drip irrigation for polyhouse farming?

    Yes, it is the best method for polyhouse crops like capsicum, cucumber, strawberry, tomato.

    What is drip irrigation?

    Drip irrigation is a system where water is supplied slowly and directly to the root zone of the plant. It saves water (up to 60–70%), increases yield, and reduces weed growth.
